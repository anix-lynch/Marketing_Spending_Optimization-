# 💼 MarketOpt: Optimizing Marketing Spending with Python

Welcome to **MarketOpt**! In this project, we’ll learn how to optimize marketing spending using **marketing mix modeling** in Python. By analyzing the relationship between sales and marketing expenses, we can make better decisions to maximize revenue generation. Using **LightweightMMM**, a powerful tool for marketing mix modeling, we’ll fine-tune models to allocate marketing budgets efficiently. Let’s optimize your marketing strategy! 🚀📈

---

## 🌐 Project Overview

Marketing mix modeling (MMM) is crucial for businesses aiming to understand how various marketing channels contribute to sales and optimize their marketing spend. In this project, we’ll dive into the fundamentals of marketing mix modeling using the **LightweightMMM** library in Python. We’ll explore the relationships between marketing expenses and sales, analyze trends, and optimize marketing budgets to achieve the best results.

---

## 🔑 Key Features

- **📊 Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM)**: Understand the key components of marketing mix modeling and its importance in optimizing spending.
- **🧮 Data Preparation**: Load, clean, and preprocess sales and marketing data for analysis.
- **📈 LightweightMMM**: Implement marketing mix models using LightweightMMM to account for trends, seasonality, and diminishing returns.
- **⚙️ Model Evaluation**: Evaluate the quality of marketing mix models by measuring correlations between sales and marketing spending.
- **🚀 Budget Optimization**: Fine-tune models to find the optimal marketing spending for each channel based on a given budget.

## 📝 Example Tasks

- **Load and Explore Data**: Use Pandas to load and clean sales and marketing data for analysis.
